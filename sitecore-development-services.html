What are Brand Guidelines?

Brand guidelines are a set of rules and standards outlining how the company’s brand will be presented to the world. Brand guidelines can be considered as a rule book for how to communicate the brand identity. The branding elements mentioned inside are used to maintain consistency.

Why every business needs brand guidelines?

To present your brand clearly

To make your brand stand out

To set up rules, standards and boundaries

To build your brand identity

To maintain internal and external consistency

To help increase brand recognition
